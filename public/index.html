Gestalt Principles for Data Visualization

TL;RD The Gestalt principles can be used to understand how people interact with and create order out of visual stimuli. There are 6 principles: Proximity: Things that are close together are perceived as belonging together. Similarity: Things that look similar are perceived as belonging together. Continuity: Our brains prefer to see smooth and continuous paths, even if there are gaps or interruptions. Closure: Our brains tend to fill in missing information to create a complete image or object....
